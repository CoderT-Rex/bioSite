Hobbies

Diana has done a number of illustrations for web comics and children’s books. This is from her first work on webtoon “Poem of Light”

Diana has been writing songs since High School. She plays the cajon, guitar, and Ukulele.

Diana enjoys video games, especially the Uncharted and Bioshock series. She plays story games with her husband and online games with her friends.

Diana has always had a passion for Japanese culture, since she was a young girl. She has studied the language and enjoys the cuisine. She took a trip there for the first time when the country reopened in 2019.

The year she got married, Diana got a Masters in Motion Graphics from Savannah College of Art and Design. She has a passion for learning and good art. She would not approve of this website.

Diana would like to have a large family. She often enjoys spending time with children, and volunteers her time doing so in Youth Ministry. She has also helped to develop children's books.
